TASK: DRAW THE BOUNDARY OF CELLS SHOWN




INSTRUCTION SET


  1. Find the image where there are biological cells
  2. Carefully draw on the boundary of the cells
  3. Press or Hold U to undo your drawing
  4. toggle Z to erase existing annotations
  5. Complete the boundaries of cells that are mostly visible
  6. Ignore the boundaries of cells that are ambigous
  7. Make sure you draw boundaries to all cells in the image
  8. Each cell should have only one boundary around it
  9. Submit your answers!
